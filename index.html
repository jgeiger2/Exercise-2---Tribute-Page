Blue Ridge Leader's School

Black Mountain, NC

Blue Ridge Leader's School (BRLS) is a yearly summer program by the YMCA. BRLS has a rigorous 6 year program. One week every year, 600 - 700 teens from all over the southeast, come togther to learn about leadership skills and physical education


BRLS changed my life forever

Here's how:

My BRLS history
